# Privacy Policy for Aura

**Effective Date:** July 10, 2026
**Last Updated:** July 10, 2026

Aura Music("we," "our," or "us") built Aura as a free music app. This Privacy
Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use
Aura.

By using Aura, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy. If you
do not agree, please do not use the app.

Aura does not require an account and does not ask for your name, email address, or
other directly identifying information. Most of your data — your library,
listening history, favorites, and downloads — stays on your device.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### Information You Provide Directly

- **Search queries:** When you search for music, radio stations, or lyrics, your
  query is sent to the corresponding service (see Section 4) to return results.

### Information Collected Automatically

- **Usage data:** With analytics enabled, we collect anonymous usage data such as
  which screens you open, searches you run, and playback sources you use. This
  data is not linked to your identity.
- **Device and diagnostic information:** We may collect device model, operating
  system version, app version, and crash/diagnostic logs to fix bugs and improve
  stability.
- **On-device analytics:** Aura also keeps a local, on-device log of app events.
  This log never leaves your device and is used only to power in-app features.

### Location Data

- Aura collects your **approximate, while-in-use location** only if you grant
  permission, and uses it solely to tune the "Mood" theming to your local weather.
  Your location is sent to Apple's WeatherKit service to retrieve current
  conditions. We do not store your location and we do not share it with anyone
  other than Apple for this weather lookup. The app works normally if you decline
  location access.

### Advertising

- Aura may display ads through Google AdMob. To serve ads, AdMob may access your
  device's advertising identifier and device information. Before any personalized
  ad tracking, Aura shows Apple's App Tracking Transparency prompt; you can decline
  tracking and continue using the app.

### Information We Do NOT Collect

- We do not require account creation to use the app.
- We do not collect your name, email, phone number, or contacts.
- We do not sell your personal information.

---

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

- Provide and maintain Aura's core functionality (search, playback, library, radio,
  lyrics, and optional Drive streaming)
- Retrieve local weather to power Mood theming
- Show and frequency-cap advertisements
- Understand aggregate usage patterns to improve the app
- Diagnose crashes and fix bugs

We do NOT use your information to:

- Sell your data to third parties
- Build cross-app advertising profiles without your consent
- Make automated decisions that significantly affect you

---

## 3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information.

We share information only in these limited circumstances:

- **Service providers:** We use the third-party services listed in Section 4 to
  operate Aura. Each receives only the data needed to perform its function.
- **Legal requirements:** We may disclose information if required by law,
  regulation, legal process, or a valid government request.
- **Safety:** We may disclose information to protect the rights, property, or
  safety of our users, the public, or us.

---

## 4. Third-Party Services

Aura relies on the following third-party services. When you use a feature powered
by one of them, the necessary data (such as a search query, an ad request, or a
weather location lookup) is sent to that provider under its own privacy policy:

| Service | Purpose | Privacy Policy |
|---------|---------|----------------|
| Google AdMob | Advertising | https://policies.google.com/privacy |
| Google Firebase Analytics | Usage & crash analytics (when enabled) | https://policies.google.com/privacy |
| Google Sign-In / Google Drive API | Optional access to your own Drive audio | https://policies.google.com/privacy |
| Various Services | Music search & streaming | https://policies.google.com/privacy |
| Apple WeatherKit | Local weather for Mood theming | https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/ |
| Radio-Browser / internet radio stations | Live radio directory & streaming | https://www.radio-browser.info/ |

Internet radio and music streams are delivered directly from third-party servers;
those providers may log standard request information (such as IP address) as part
of delivering the stream, subject to their own policies.

---

## 5. Data Retention

- **On-device data** (library, history, favorites, downloads, on-device analytics
  log): retained on your device until you delete it in the app or uninstall Aura.
- **Analytics and crash data** shared with Firebase: retained according to
  Google's Firebase data-retention settings, then deleted or anonymized.
- **Advertising data**: handled by Google AdMob per Google's policies.

Uninstalling Aura removes all data stored locally on your device.

---

## 6. Data Security

We implement reasonable technical measures to protect your information, including:

- Encryption in transit (TLS/HTTPS) for app service traffic
- On-device storage of your library and preferences
- Requesting only the permissions and third-party access the app needs

Note: some internet-radio streams are only available over plain HTTP; those
specific media streams are not encrypted in transit because the stations do not
offer HTTPS. All app service and API traffic uses HTTPS.

No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, but we take commercially
reasonable steps to protect your data.

## 7. Your Rights

Because Aura does not maintain user accounts, most of your data is under your
direct control on your device — you can delete your history, favorites, downloads,
cached streams, and the on-device analytics log from within the app at any time,
and you can disconnect Google Drive at any time.

To ask questions or make a privacy request, contact us at kocakmstf@gmail.com. We
will respond within 30 days.

### For European Union Residents (GDPR)

If you are in the European Economic Area, you have rights under the GDPR:

- **Data controller:** Aura Music, bilgisiz1mail@gmail.com
- **Lawful basis for processing:**
  - Your consent (analytics, location, personalized ad tracking — each of which
    you can decline or withdraw)
  - Performance of a contract (providing the features you request)
  - Legitimate interests (app stability, security, and improvement)
- **Your rights:** access, rectification, erasure, restriction of processing,
  objection to processing, and data portability.
- **Complaints:** You may lodge a complaint with your local Data Protection
  Authority.
- **International transfers:** Third-party providers (e.g., Google, Apple) may
  process data outside the EEA under appropriate safeguards such as Standard
  Contractual Clauses.

### For California Residents (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have rights under the CCPA:

- **Right to know:** request the categories and specific pieces of personal
  information collected about you.
- **Right to delete:** request deletion of your personal information.
- **Right to opt-out:** We do not sell your personal information. You may decline
  ad tracking via the App Tracking Transparency prompt.
- **Non-discrimination:** We will not discriminate against you for exercising your
  rights.

To exercise these rights, contact us at bilgisiz1mail@gmail.com.
